What I do

I help you expand on your musical ideas by adding the right parts to your songs. Those parts can be rhythm or lead tracks on electric guitar, acoustic guitar, bass guitar, lapsteel, or any combination of the above. I can also help with arrangements and work up great drum parts in Logic Pro X, or provide access to real, human drummers.

Overdubs

Your music is complete, but you'd like certain tracks professionally re-recorded

Part Writing

Your music has the basics and you would like to add instrumental parts or a full band

Arrangement

You have ideas for themes, or parts, but you need help with arrangements & progressions

Collaboration

You need a writing partner to work back & forth with to develop your musical ideas
